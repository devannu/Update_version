Self Visitor Registration

  • Enter your personal details

  • 2nd scan → Exit marked
  • Dashboard updates in real-time

  • Export full visitor log as PDF

Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: How does the Visitor Management System work?

The visitor fills a form, generates a QR code, and security scans it to mark check-in & check-out timings.

Q2: Who uses this system?

Visitors for registration, security guards for scanning, and admin for records & monitoring.

Q3: How is visitor entry verified?

Security guards scan the visitor’s QR code — first scan marks entry and second scan marks exit.
